Art of the day
🎨 Title : Snuff Bottle
👤 Artist: Unknown
🗓️ Date  : second half of the 19th century
Summary  : Snuff Bottle is a Snuff bottle from the China culture, created around second half of the 19th century. It is part of the Asian Art collection at the Met. The piece is classified as Snuff Bottles and was made using Green tourmaline, the interior stained blue, with pink tourmaline stopper, wood stand.

📸 Information from https://collectionapi.metmuseum.org
