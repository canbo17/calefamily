Sid the Science Kid, also known as Jim Henson's Sid the Science Kid, is an American children's animated educational television series produced by The Jim Henson Company in association with PBS affiliate KCET, that aired on PBS Kids from September 1, 2008 to November 15, 2012. The show is created using digital puppetry.
